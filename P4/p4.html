Calculate REM ?

REM stands for Rapid Eye Movement and occurs during sleep in cycles of about 90-120 minutes throughout the night. It is best to wake up inbetween REM cycles, the lightest point of sleep. Waking up between REM cycles will leave you feeling refreshed and less groggy than if you were to wake up in the middle of a REM cycle.
